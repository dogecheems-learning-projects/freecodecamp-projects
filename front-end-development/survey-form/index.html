ACOMSS Umbrella Organizations | Survey Form

In this form, you will be asked whether you would like to join under an umbrella organization inside Adamson Computer Science Society. The list of umbrella organizations in ACOMSS are as follows:

P.U.L.S.E.: This organization is a community dedicated to empowering aspiring machine learning and software engineers.

OPUS: This organization is responsible for the editorial endeavors of the Adamson Computer Science Society. A collaborative team of young creatives specializing in crafting publications like posters, brochures, infographics, pamphlets, and literary works.

Haraya: This organization is responsible for event coverage within the ACOMSS department through photography and videography. Comprising diverse creatives in Multimedia Arts fields such as photography, videography, and video editing, this organization captures the essence of events.
